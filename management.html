MANAGEMENT



If the technical teams are the mind of the club the management team is the heart.

Any organization, institution, forum or club cannot solely depend on the thinking minds for Its progress, there has to be someone to manage everything. Our tasks can often be as challenging and complex as the fabrication and designing of the AUV. For this we have the Management team, that ensures a hassle free working experience for the club.

The team helps in organizing events, workshops, meeting etcs that promote our club activities. Here the social media content is created and updated for various platforms like Facebook, YouTube and Instagram. We're also responsible for the financing of the club by procuring funds and gathering sponsors. We manage and allocate the funds prudently. The management team is the interface of the club and the corporate world. It gives members a glimpse of what Its like and what it takes to handle one the most important task of getting funds for the club.
