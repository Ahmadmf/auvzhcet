Donate for Kerela

The recent floods in Kerala have severely devasted vast regions of the state. Thousands have been displaced and are in need of urgent help. HELP REBUILD KERLA NOW! It is a collaboration with SOCH NGO, AUV ZHCET Club, Roboclub & SAE ZHCET. The donation boxes will be placed at Main building & NL Block on 1st Sept. 2018.
